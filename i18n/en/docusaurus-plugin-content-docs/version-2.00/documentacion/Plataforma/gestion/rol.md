The **Roles** section allows you to manage different access profiles within the platform. From here, you can create, edit, and configure the permissions and user experiences that each role will have.

## Roles View

A list of all available roles on the platform is presented. When one is selected, its detailed configuration is displayed.
![listado](/img/gestion/roles/lista.png)

---

## Assigned Users

Upon entering a role, you can see which users are part of it, facilitating access control by group or profile.
![listado](/img/gestion/roles/usuarios.png)

---

## Configure Home Page

Each role can have a **custom home page**, meaning the section users will be directed to when logging into Daiana.

![listado](/img/gestion/roles/inicio.png)

:::::info
If the **Chat** window is selected as the home page, 4 additional actions that can be configured are enabled:

- **Main chat view**
- **Manage chats**
- **Last open conversation**
- **Go to a specific chat**
:::::

---

## Enable or Disable Sections

From this view, you can also **activate or deactivate tabs** of the platform for all members of the role, limiting their access to certain sections according to operational needs or internal policies.

![listado](/img/gestion/roles/pantallas.png)

:::::info
This functionality allows customizing the experience of each user type, improving usability and security.
:::::
