The **Statistics** section of the Dashboard allows you to clearly and segmentally visualize how the **Daiana** platform is used in your organization. It provides historical information, rankings, and customizable metrics to facilitate tracking usage, assistant performance, and user and team engagement.
<Banerimg img="dashboard/total.gif" />

## Monthly Ranking

<p align="center">
![top users](/img/dashboard/rank.png)
</p>

1. **Filter:** Filter by month to consult the ranking activity for that period.
2. **Total Submissions:** Shows the total messages sent in the selected period.
3. **Top 5 users** with most activity
4. **Top 5 most used assistants**

::::tip
Useful for evaluating recent adoption and month-to-month performance of agents and users.
:::::

### Filter
<p align="center">

![Month Filter](/img/dashboard/filtromes.png)

</p>

1. **Show**: last 12 months, last 6 months, or specific period.
2. **View by:** calendar month or contracted period.
3. **Teams**: filter by one or more specific teams.

:::::info
This section helps identify trends and seasonality, as well as measure the impact of new implementations.
:::::
