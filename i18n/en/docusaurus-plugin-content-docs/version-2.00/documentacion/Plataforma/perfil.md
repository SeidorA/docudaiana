The **Profile** section allows each user to personalize their experience in Daiana and manage personal data easily and securely.

---

## General Information

<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/general.png)
</p>

The left column contains the form to modify the following data:

- **Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Phone**
- **Email**

After making modifications, it is necessary to press the **"Update"** button to save the changes.

---

## Preferences
<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/preferencias.png)
</p>

Within the same side panel, you can customize the platform's user experience:

- **Change language:** select the interface language from the available options (e.g., Spanish or English).
- **Change theme:** switch between **light** or **dark** mode according to your visual preference.

---

## My Summary
<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/resumen.png)
</p>
The central part displays an informative summary of the user:

- **Assigned Role**
- **Number of work groups**
- **Number of linked assistants**

---

## Work Teams

<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/equipo.png)
</p>

View all the **teams** you belong to. Each team is represented by a card with its name, identifying color, and description.

---

## Linked Assistants

<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/asistente.png)
</p>

A list of agents to which the user has access is displayed. Each assistant includes:

- Custom image or avatar
- Agent name
- Corresponding administrator group

---

## Home Screen

<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/inicio.png)
</p>

The user can choose which will be the **default screen** upon entering Daiana (e.g., Dashboard, Agent, Knowledge Base, etc.).

Once selected, press **"Save"** for the configuration to take effect.

---

## Security

<p align="center">
![a](/img/perfil/seguridad.png)
</p>

In this section, you can update your password:

- Enter your **current password**
- Type your **new password**
- Confirm the changes by clicking **"Update"**

:::::info
This section ensures that each user has full control over their profile and environment within the platform.
:::::
